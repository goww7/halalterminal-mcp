## Why this fits the list

There is currently **no MCP server** in this list (or anywhere in the ecosystem, AFAIK) for Shariah-compliant / Islamic / halal investing — a research surface ~1.8B people would benefit from and a category that sits awkwardly between general-finance MCPs (which don't apply Islamic-finance rules) and trading-broker MCPs (which the audience usually can't use).

What it does:
- Screens stocks and ETFs across **5 Shariah methodologies** (AAOIFI, DJIM, FTSE, MSCI, S&P)
- Audits portfolios (per-stock verdict + aggregate compliant % + purification owed)
- Computes **zakat** (2.5% obligatory charity) and **dividend purification**
- Bulk-screens entire indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ-100, FTSE 100, …)
- Pulls market data, news, and SEC EDGAR XBRL facts

Apache-2.0 license. Free API tier (50 tokens/month, ~5–10 full screenings, no credit card). Works in Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, Cline, Continue, Zed, and any other MCP-compatible client.
